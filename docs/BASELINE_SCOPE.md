Baseline Scope (Frozen)

Baseline family B:
- Weisfeiler–Leman color refinement (1-WL)

Excluded (deferred):
- k-WL for k >= 2
- Sampled or truncated WL variants
- SDP, spectral, or ML baselines

Rationale:
URF-minimal obstruction is defined relative to B.
Stronger baselines are out of scope for this certificate.
